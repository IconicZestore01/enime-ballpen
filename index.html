Naruto Summary.

"Naruto" is a Japanese anime and manga series created by Masashi Kishimoto. It follows the story of a young ninja named Naruto Uzumaki, who dreams of becoming the strongest ninja in his village, known as the Hokage, and gaining the respect of others. Naruto was born with a powerful beast called the Nine-Tails Fox (Kurama) sealed inside him, which caused the villagers to fear and avoid him. Despite a lonely childhood, Naruto remains determined, cheerful, and never gives up.

Zoro Summary.

Roronoa Zoro is one of the main characters in the anime and manga series One Piece, created by Eiichiro Oda. He is the first crew member to join Monkey D. Luffys pirate crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, and serves as their swordsman.

Blackclober Summary.

Black Clover is a Japanese anime and manga series written by Yūki Tabata. The story takes place in a magical world where everyone is born with the ability to use magic—everyone except the main character, Asta.
